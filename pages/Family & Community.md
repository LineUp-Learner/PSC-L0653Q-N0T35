# #Family

*"Love and Dharma are the flower and fruit of family life"*
								*- Tiruvalluvar*

**FAMILY** - The earliest and one of the most fundamental unit of society.

**Joint Family** - Several generations living together
**Nuclear Family** - Generally a couple, optionally with their children and their parents

**Significance of Family and #relationships in India** - Indian Languages have many terms denoting relationships. This signifies the importance to family and relationships.

Some values and morals that family teach: Dhaana(Charity), Dharma(Morality), Ahimsa(Non-violence), Tyaaga(sacrifice), Seva (Service), Saving, #Participation, Humbleness, respect, planning, discipline, authority, inclusivity, responsibility, support, sharing and caring, #cooperation etc.

# #Community 

**COMMUNITY** - Connection and #Coordination of families and people with other families and people around them. Communities are always ultimately inter-dependent. 

**Communal Gathering** - Festivals, feats, funeral, wedding, harvest, sowing, etc.

**Inter-community Gathering** - For planning and agreement on mutual interests and benefits across different communities, i.e., Utilisation of land and water resources, agricultural produce etc. Ex: #rural, #tribal, #urban communities

#Community - Also denotes a group of people of a religion/common work/interests.
