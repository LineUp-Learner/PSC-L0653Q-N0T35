## Constitutional Framework

- #Article_21A : #Right_to_Education as a #fundamental_right #RTE
- #Article_45 : Provision for early #childhood_care and #childhood_education for children below 6 years
- 86th #Constitutional_Amendment_Act, 2002: Made education a #fundamental_right
- #Article_51A(k): Fundamental duty of parents to provide educational opportunities

## Directive Principles

- #Article_39(f): Protection of children from exploitation and moral abandonment
- #Article_46 : Promotion of educational interests of #weaker_sections #EWS

## Key Legislative Framework

### 1. #Right_to_Education Act ( #RTE ), 2009

- **Key Provisions**
    - Free and compulsory #education for children aged 6-14 years
    - 25% #reservation in #private #school for disadvantaged groups
    - #infrastructure norms and teacher qualifications
    - #No_detention_policy till Class 8
    - Prohibition of screening procedures and capitation fees
- **Implementation Mechanisms**
    - #Continuous_and_Comprehensive_Evaluation ( #CCE )
    - Community participation through #School_Management_Committees ( #SMC )
    - #Pupil-Teacher_Ratio #PTR maintenance
    - Teacher Qualification Requirements and Maintenance

### 2. #National_Education_Policy , 2020 #NEP

- \*\*Early Childhood care and Childhood Education ( #ECCE )
    - Integration of #ECCE to formal school education
    - Universal access to quality #ECCE
    - **National Curricular Framework** for **ECCE**
    - **Strengthening of #Anganwadi system**
    - Better Integration of **ICDS**
- **New pedagogical structure (5+3+3+4)** - (Foundational/2nd + Preparatory/5th + Middle/8th + Secondary/12th)
- **Experiential and Experimental** learning
- **Art and Sports**-integrated education
- **Integration of #pre-school_education with formal schooling**
- Emphasis on foundational literacy and numeracy
- Integration of **vocational education**
- **Multiple entry/exit** options

## Major Schemes and Initiatives

### 1. #Sarva_Shiksha_Abhiyan ( #SSA )

- Focuses on universalizing elementary education and bridging gender and social gaps in education.

### 2. #Rashtriya_Madhyamik_Shiksha_Abhiyan ( #RMSA )

- Universalization of secondary education (ages 14-18)
- Improve access to quality secondary education.
- Reduce dropout rates at the secondary level.
- Enhance the quality of education through teacher training, curriculum development, and infrastructure development.

### 3. #Samagra_Shiksha_Abhiyan ( #SSA )

- Integrated scheme for #school #education (Pre-school to Class 12)
- Components:
    - Infrastructure development
    - Quality enhancement
    - Teacher training
    - #Digital_Education
    - #Gender and #Social #inclusivity

### 3. #Mid-Day_Meal Scheme ( #PM_POSHAN )

- Nutritional support to primary school children
- Coverage: Government and government-aided schools
- Impact:
    - Increased enrolment
    - Reduced #drop-out rates
    - Improved #nutrition levels
    - Better social #integration

### 4. #Integrated_Child_Development_Services ( #ICDS )

- #Anganwadi system for children below 6 years
- Services:
    - #Supplementary_nutrition
    - Pre-school education
    - #Health check-ups
    - #Immunization
    - Referral services

### 5. #NIPUN Bharat Mission

- Focus on foundational #literacy and #numeracy
- Target: Universal foundational literacy by 2026-27
- Key components:
    - Activity-based learning
    - Capacity building of teachers
    - Learning outcome #frameworks
    - #Assessment tools

### 6. Digital Initiatives

- #DIKSHA Portal
    - #Digital content and teacher training
    - QR-coded textbooks
    - Online assessments
- #PM_e-VIDYA #e-VIDYA
    - Educational TV channels
    - Radio broadcasting through #Community radio
    - One class, one channel through #SWAYAM_PRABHA #SWAYAM
    - Digital education access

## Special Focus Areas

### 1. Girl Child Education

- **BETI BACHAO BETI PADHAO (2014)**  #Beti_Bachao_Beti_Padhao
    - Comprehensive campaign for girl child education
    - Multi-sectoral intervention
    - Community engagement
- **Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalaya** #KGBV
    - Residential schools for girls from disadvantaged groups
    - Upper primary level education
    - Focus on SC/ST/OBC/minority communities
- **National Scheme of Incentives to Girls for Secondary Education #NSIGSE:** Provides financial incentives to girls who pass Class 8 and enrol in Class 9, aiming to reduce dropout rates among girls at the secondary level.

### 2. #Inclusive Education

- #Scholarship for #SC/ST students
- Pre-matric scholarships for #minorities
- Special educators for **children with disabilities**
- Transport and escort facilities
- Programs for Children with Special Needs
- Scheme for **Infrastructure Development in Minority Institutions**
- Modernization of #Madrasas

### 3. Quality Enhancement

- Learning Enhancement Programme
- School Quality #Assessment and #Accreditation Framework
- Performance Grading Index #PGI
- National Initiative for School Heads and Teachers' Holistic Advancement
