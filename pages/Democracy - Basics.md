**People #vote their #Representatives for national ( #Member_of_Parliament ) or state ( #Member_of_Legislative_Assembly ) level assemblies through #Voting #Elections , where they discuss problems, make decisions, pass laws & bills, etc.**

#Voting - Process through which every citizen's opinion is taken to finalize the representatives to form the government. Also, called #Indirect_Democracy . A democracy that follows this process is called #Representative_Democracy .
#Grassroot_Democracy - A system that enables & encourages participation of all the citizens to have vote/say on the decisions that affect them.
